About Us

Startup & Tech Mixer is an organization that creates dynamic ways for members of unique communities to authentically connect based on shared interests, passions, and values. By harnessing the power of ingenuity and human connection, the event platform cultivates a socially-inspired movement to redefine networking. Each element of the format reflects the best of conferences, pitches, exhibitions, and parties to create a thoughtful and fun experience. Participants are encouraged to interact with the best and brightest of today’s innovators, from CEOs and founders to developers and engineers, in consciously designed spaces that are built to foster opportunity.

Our Mission

Startup & Tech Mixer's Mission is to inspire and nurture the inner entrepreneur and leader in all of us. We foster innovation by taking something that is typically transactional into an experience that's transformational.
